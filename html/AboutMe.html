About me
i’m a Seattle-based web developer with a passion for blending creativity and technology. I graduated summa cum laude from the University of Washington with a degree in Computer Science, and since then, I’ve been fortunate to work on a wide range of projects that challenge me to think outside the box. Whether it’s building a sleek portfolio for an artist or developing a full-fledged e-commerce platform, I love finding solutions that are both beautiful and functional.

My journey into web development started unexpectedly. During a gap year traveling through Europe, I started a travel blog. I spent hours tweaking the design and writing custom code until I realized that I loved the process of building the website even more than writing the blog posts themselves. That experience ignited my love for web development, and I haven’t looked back since.
When I’m not coding, you’ll usually find me out on a hiking trail with my camera in hand. Living in the Pacific Northwest, I love exploring hidden trails and capturing the beauty of nature. Hiking helps me clear my mind, and photography allows me to bring a bit of that natural inspiration into my designs. The mountains, trees, and open skies are my happy place, and they often influence the colors and patterns in my web development work.
